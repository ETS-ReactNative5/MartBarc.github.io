Top-down shooter prototype.
Why is mouse tracking hard?

Constructed using the UnrealEngine 4, me and some friends constructed a mini top-down twin stick shooter. The controls use both keyboard and mouse inputs, turning the player character requires mouse tracking which the coordinates is translated into the game. Force, from the exploding enemies, can move some of the objects around. Left clicking fires a projectile that can hit enemy AI (very dumb zombie AI) and deal damage. Basic UI can be seen as well as basic lighting and textures. Its a start :P.
